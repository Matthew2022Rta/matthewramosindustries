Awards & Recognition

NASA L'SPACE Mission Concept Academy

Date: Spring 2025

Role: Electrical Engineer — Team VULCAN

Organization: NASA L'SPACE Program

Overview

Selected for NASA's L'SPACE Mission Concept Academy, where I served as an Electrical Engineer on Team VULCAN, designing the power system — supply, generation, and distribution — for a preliminary Venus-exploration mission concept. Working through NASA's real mission lifecycle, our multidisciplinary team delivered a full Preliminary Design Review (PDR), and I gained hands-on experience in systems engineering, mission requirements development, and CAD modeling.

Skill Badges Earned

  • Teaming
  • Systems Engineering
  • Requirements Development
  • Risk Mitigation
  • Heat Transfer
  • CAD Design (Siemens NX)
